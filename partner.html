Partner Training

Tech training is a key for tech partners and resellers that provide services to end customers.

Utimaco offers extensive hands-on training to give installers, administrators, operators, and auditors the skills and confidence necessary to work with the Utimaco Lawful Interception Management System in complex network environments. The training program is specifically designed to meet the needs of engineers who install and administrate the Utimaco LIMS system as well as for operators and auditors who use the LIMS Software to support their usual course of business.

In addition, Utimaco provides trainings to partners to help them identify customer requirements and define state-of-the-art lawful intercept and data retention solutions for their telecom networks.
